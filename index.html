DR. ERIC HOSKINS
Director

Dr. Hoskins is a medical doctor with a Ph.D. in public health and epidemiology from Oxford University and has spent nearly four decades in the healthcare sector. A Rhodes Scholar and an Officer of the Order of Canada (Canada’s highest civilian award), Eric has worked in health systems in Canada and around the world. From 2014-18, he was Ontario, Canada’s Minister of Health in charge of North America’s largest health system, and prior to that was Minister of Economic Development and Trade, Minister of Children and Youth Services, and Minister of Citizenship and Immigration. He is currently a Partner at Maverix Private Equity in Toronto and serves on the boards of several publicly-traded companies.
